Thank you for downloading the Table Planner.

The Table Planner is a cross-platform multi-device system (means that your Android and Apple users can share the same data) running in the cloud that will robust your restaurant reservations control.

Here's how you should get started:

  • By setting your tables and activity hours
  • By changing your business name and logo
  • By adding your Hosts (Optional, if you need your staff to share the main reservation board)
  • By setting your language
  • By editing your personal SMS message for clients confirmations
